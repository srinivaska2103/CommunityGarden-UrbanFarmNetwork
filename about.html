Our Roots

It started with a single seed and a simple question.

01 The Concrete Problem

Cities were losing their soul.

In the heart of Salem, we saw a growing disconnect. Between concrete and busy streets, there was no room for nature. Fresh, organic food felt like a luxury, not a right.

2024

Where it began

02 The Green Solution

Reclaiming the space.

We started with one abandoned lot. We cleared the debris and invited neighbors. Within months, that "empty space" was feeding ten families.

03 The Growing Network

A movement that won't stop.

Today, GreenRoot is a thriving network of 12 community gardens. We're growing friendships and resilience for the next generation.
