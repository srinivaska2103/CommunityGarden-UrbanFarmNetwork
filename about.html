Our Roots

It started with a single seed and a simple question.

01 The Concrete Problem

Cities were losing their soul.

In the heart of Salem, we saw a growing disconnect. Between towering concrete and busy streets, there was no room for nature—and even less room for the community to breathe. Fresh, organic food felt like a luxury, not a right.

2024

Where it began

02 The Green Solution

Reclaiming the space.

We didn't wait for permission to change the city—we started with one abandoned lot. We cleared the debris, enriched the soil, and invited the neighbors. Within months, that "empty space" was providing enough tomatoes and greens to feed ten families.

03 The Growing Network

A movement that won't stop.

What began as a hobby became a revolution. Today, GreenRoot is a thriving network of 12 community gardens across 3 cities. We aren't just growing food; we're growing friendships, resilience, and a sustainable future for the next generation.
